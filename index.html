Introduction

I consider myself to be a self-taught programmer. I have a degree in Computer Science degree plus a 5+ years of experience in the field programming. My focus was software Web development. The code I wrote outside of class and work for fun was more evolved than in class and work.

As I remember from my last review I was asked to check out design patterns. My reply was that I design with my own code style. However, the patterns that I read about or came across, some of them I was actually applying but I didn't realize their name or concept.
